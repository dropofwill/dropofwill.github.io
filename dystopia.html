Storyboards for (Dys)topia's Opening Title Sequence

For this project I created storyboards for an opening title sequence for a fictional TV show. My concept was a tv drama that focuses on the everyday lives of people living in an oppresive society. For the title sequence I portrayed the characters and setting as clean and warm to represent how these people try to view their surroundings, but I used the lights and shadows to reveal the sinister undertone of the world they actually live in. Created with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
