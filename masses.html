The Masses vs. the Individual Photography Project

The assignment was to photograph two compositions of the same scene, but alter it in such a way that they portray two completely different moods and/or messages. I chose to shoot origami dragons that I folded as the subject matter and the masses versus the individual as my message. In the first shot I set them up in formation to represent the masses blindly following each other along in a line and in the second shot I set one of them out standing up against the masses. Shot with a Cannon EOS Digital Rebel XT with an 18.0-55.0mm Lens, edited with Adobe Photoshop.
