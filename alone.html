Alone Interactive Story Book

This project is an interactive story book app for Edgar Allan Poe's poem Alone. Through the use of interactive sounds, imagery, and typography, I created a creepy and engaging atmosphere for the poem to be experienced. Created with Adobe Flash + ActionScript 3, Illustrator, Photoshop, and Audacity. Try it Out →
