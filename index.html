Storyboards for (Dys)topia's Opening Title Sequence

For this project I created storyboards for an opening title sequence for a fictional TV show. My concept was a tv drama that focuses on the everyday lives of people living in an oppresive society. For the title sequence I portrayed the characters and setting as clean and warm to represent how these people try to view their surroundings, but I used the lights and shadows to reveal the sinister undertone of the world they actually live in.Created with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. Lenore 404 Page

This project is a mockup web page for a fictional company demonstrated through a 404 page. This involved making both the material for the error page and the global elements of the site. The company I came up with was a creative development agency named Lenore, inc. The material for the 404 page is a play on Edgar Allan Poe's poem The Raven.
